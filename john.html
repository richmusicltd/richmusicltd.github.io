About - John Cardillo

John is currently the host of Newsmax TV’s America Talks Live.

A Queens native, John Cardillo patrolled the south Bronx as a member of the NYPD. After serving the citizens of New York, John fused his law enforcement experience and business acumen to found a highly successful online safety and security firm.

His extensive criminal justice and policing experience led to him be called on by Attorneys General and industry coalitions. John helped block or pass legislation in over a dozen states and has testified as an expert witness and subject matter expert before the United States Congress and over a dozen state legislatures. He was appointed a member of Harvard Law School’s prestigious Internet Safety Technical Task Force, and in 2008 was the recipient of a tribute from the Florida House of Representatives for his contributions.

John began his own career in conservative media as a contributor to Glenn Beck and The Blaze, then launching a weekend show on 1290 WJNO, The Palm Beaches, and later hosting the eponymous John Cardillo Show on Salem Media Group’s WZAB 880 “The Biz” in Miami/Ft. Lauderdale.

In 2016 John was included in Talkers Magazine’s Heavy Hundred List, which recognizes the 100 most influential talk radio hosts in America.

John moved over to TV as a frequent analyst and guest for various national news programs, and as a regular contributor to Dr. Drew on HLN.

In early 2018 John was asked by Newsmax TV to helm the 2PM hour.
